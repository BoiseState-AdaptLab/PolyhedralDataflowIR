\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A tuple of expressions. 

Modeled somewhat off of \mbox{\hyperlink{classiegenlib_1_1_u_f_call_term}{U\+F\+Call\+Term}} but also off \mbox{\hyperlink{classiegenlib_1_1_tuple_decl}{Tuple\+Decl}} in terms of not exposing how expressions are stored.

Memory management\+: the \mbox{\hyperlink{classiegenlib_1_1_tuple_exp_term}{Tuple\+Exp\+Term}} assumes ownership of any expressions it contains, copies those when the \mbox{\hyperlink{classiegenlib_1_1_tuple_exp_term}{Tuple\+Exp\+Term}} itself is copied, and deletes them when the \mbox{\hyperlink{classiegenlib_1_1_tuple_exp_term}{Tuple\+Exp\+Term}} is destroyed.
