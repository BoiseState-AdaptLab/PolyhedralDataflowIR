\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Presburger \mbox{\hyperlink{classomega_1_1_formula}{Formula}} base class. 

A multi-\/way(0 allowed) tree that represents a Presburger formula. Children can be either other formula nodes or \char`\"{}atomic\char`\"{} constraints (single equality, inequality, or stride).
