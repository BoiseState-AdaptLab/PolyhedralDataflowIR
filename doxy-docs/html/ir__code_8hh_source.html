5  Purpose:
6  CHiLL's compiler intermediate representation interface that extends
7  Omega's builder interface to accomodate compiler analyses and
8  extra code generation.
9 .
10  Notes:
11  Unlike CG_outputRepr, IR_Symbol, IR_Ref and IR_Control are place holders
12  to the underlying code, thus deleting or duplicating them does not affect
13  the actual code. Similar to Omega builder's memory allocation strategy,
14  all non-const pointer parameters of CG_outputRepr/IR_Symbol/IR_Ref/IR_Control
15  are destroyed after the call.
