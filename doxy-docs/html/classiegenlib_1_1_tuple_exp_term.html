Detailed Description

A tuple of expressions.

Modeled somewhat off of UFCallTerm but also off TupleDecl in terms of not exposing how expressions are stored.

Memory management: the TupleExpTerm assumes ownership of any expressions it contains, copies those when the TupleExpTerm itself is copied, and deletes them when the TupleExpTerm is destroyed.
